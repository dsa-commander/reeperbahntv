The Author

DEAN
BRIAN HALL

"The Reeperbahn isn't just a street. It's a living archive — every building, every doorway, every name above a venue tells a story that's been half-forgotten."

Dean Brian Hall was born on 29 June 1978 in Wallsend, Newcastle-Upon-Tyne, England. A linguist by training — holding a BA in Modern Languages (German & Spanish) from the University of Northumbria and a PGCE from Liverpool John Moores University — Dean spent several years working as a translator in Barcelona before relocating to Germany in 2008.

A chance visit to Hamburg in 2012 changed everything. The Reeperbahn, with its layered, contradictory history, became his obsession. What followed was over five years of meticulous archival research, walking every centimetre of the street, interviewing locals, and piecing together the district's story location by location.

His debut book, Reeperbahn Tour: A Location History of an Entertainment District, was published in 2022. A second volume is in progress. His YouTube channel, ReeperbahnTV, continues to document the street's present and past through short-form documentary filmmaking.

He is fluent in German, Spanish, and Catalan, and is based in Hamburg.
